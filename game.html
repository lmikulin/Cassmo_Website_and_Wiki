System Requirements

DISCLAIMER: The game has not been tested on a wide range of hardware, it may or may not work on less or more, so try at your own risk


Minimum System Specifications

  • Operating System: Windows Vista/7/8
  • Processor: 2GHz or better
  • Memory: 4GB RAM
  • Graphics: GTX8800 or better (256MB VRAM)
  • Network: Broadband Internet
  • Hard Drive: 5GB or more available space
  • Sound Card: Need a sound card
  • Other: Keyboard and Mouse required

Recommended System Specifications

  • Operating System: Windows 7/8
  • Processor: Quad Core 2.4GHz or better
  • Memory: 4GB RAM
  • Graphics: GTX480 or better (1gb VRAM)
  • Network: Broadband Internet
  • Hard Drive: 5GB or more availible space
  • Sound Card: Need a sound card
  • Other: Keyboard and Mouse required
